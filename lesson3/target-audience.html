Target Audience

Our target audience are outdoor people who likes to enjoy cold season, practice winter sports and all the regional events during the fall and winter season. This audience is between 18 and 60 years old, active persons and adventurous, they like to connect with other people who has the same interests and hobbies like them.

Jonatan

Jonatan is a 40 years old, he's planning to go on vacation with his family and is looking for a location where they can enjoy Christmas spirit. Also, he wants to know when will be the best time to travel en enjoy winter weather.

Alejandra

Alejandra has 27 years old, single. She lives in a very hot place and enjoys all kind of outdoor activities. Now she's looking to experiment outdoor life in a better weather and live new experiences.
